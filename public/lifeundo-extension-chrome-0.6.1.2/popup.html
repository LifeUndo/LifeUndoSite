            Frequently asked questions
            What is GetLifeUndo?
            GetLifeUndo is a family of apps (desktop, browser extensions, mobile clients) that keeps a local timeline of your work: tabs, text forms and screenshots. In one click you can restore a whole tab session, return text into a form or open the right screenshot — even if the browser crashed, the profile broke or the app was removed.
            How does it work?
            The extension and desktop app capture tab, form and screenshot events into local LifeUndo journals. In the Timeline there are buttons like Restore all tabs from session, Restore selected and Restore this version for text: you can reopen 1–1000 tabs for the needed session, return text into a form or open a screenshot directly from a local folder. TEAM and mobile clients use a shared data format so that sync between devices can be enabled later.
            Where is data stored?
            All data is stored locally on the device. The browser extension uses the browser profile storage, and the desktop and mobile clients use their own local LifeUndo folders. Nothing is sent to GetLifeUndo servers unless you explicitly enable sync. Exact paths and history size can be checked in the desktop app settings.
            Do I need to configure anything?
            By default everything works locally and automatically: the log of tabs, forms and screenshots is written to local storage without sign-up and without cloud. In the desktop client you can configure history depth, backup format and enable TEAM. Cloud sync for office/home and choosing a specific cloud (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive or LifeUndo Cloud) are planned as a separate option and are disabled by default.
            What if I cancel my subscription?
            Local access to already saved data remains: you can still open old tabs, text and screenshots from your LifeUndo folders. New events (tabs, forms, screenshots) will not be captured until the subscription is renewed. For a limited time we may occasionally remind you about payment, but the choice is always yours.
            Office/home: how does sync work?
            By default nothing is sent anywhere: each computer writes its own history of tabs, text and screenshots only locally. The office/home and TEAM modes are designed so that several devices (home PC, work laptop, phone) can see a shared timeline via secure exchange between clients. This is planned to use a user-chosen cloud or LifeUndo Cloud; if the internet is lost everything continues to be written locally and is synchronised later. In TEAM the admin manages the list of devices and policies, and browser and desktop clients act as devices of one account.
